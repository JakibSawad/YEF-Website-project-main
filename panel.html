EXECUTIVE-BODY MEMBERS

TERM 2024-2025

Afsana Ahmed

President

Her unassailable charisma and sheer talent has made her not only a sensation in the Academic department, but a role model for YEF. Her talents and values embody the ethos of the club, underlying the growth and innovation of the club with her at the helm.

Rafid Khandaker

Vice-President

He writes like no other. Not only does his rhythmic flow of thoughts captivate, but his humble presence and the eloquence with which he delivers everything, is truly mind-blowing.

Minhazur Rahman

General Secretary

A dedicated and active leader, his passion and punctuality have been vital to the IT department’s success. His exceptional graphic designing skills and hard work enhance his character, making him an invaluable asset whose actions truly speak louder than words.

Ashraful Alam Asir

Treasurer

His expertise in matters related to the admin and logistics core make him an indispensable figurehead for the club. With his calm, collected and humble demeanour as well as great leadership skills, he brings everyone together and maintains harmony in the club.

Labiba Fairooz Khan

Joint Secretary

A true powerhouse in every endeavor she uptakes, she leads with values, ensuring every voice is heard. Her exceptional communication skills and thoughtful leadership continue to shape the future of the Young Economists' Forum.

As the term 2023-2024 concludes and we welcome a new chapter for our cherished club, it is time to unveil the distinguished squad that will carry the torch of leadership for YEF. They belong to the select group of individuals who have inscribed their names in the legacy of our esteemed club as some of the most devoted, enthusiastic, and talented members. Beginning their journey as Junior Executive Members, they have endured challenges and demonstrated their merit in rising to their current positions. United, they create the foundation that supports this lively community and act as role models for future generations. The Young Economists' Forum extends its heartfelt congratulations to the Executive Body for the term 2024-2025 and warmly welcomes them as they take charge and guide us toward new and exciting possibilities.

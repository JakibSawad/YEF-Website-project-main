About

Established in 1998, Young Economists’ Forum is the first and only academic club of North South University. As the sole academic club at North South University, YEF stands out for its unique approach and it acts as a sanctuary young budding talent. For years, YEF has upheld its prestige, demonstrated its calibre, and maintained its moral standards through the countless remarkable contributions of all its alumni, members and also the faculty members of the university. With an immensely talented and hard-working member body, vibrant events and exclusive publications, YEF is all set to welcome its new recruits who would bring theories to life!

Events

EconProdigy:

YEF’s flagship event, EconProdigy, is the nation’s largest inter-university policy-making competition. Having completed six successful seasons, it is now preparing for an exciting new edition. With thousands of participants over the years, it serves as a premier platform for young policy-makers to showcase their talents and drive meaningful change. The competition challenges participants to craft innovative policies addressing pressing economic and social issues, guided by a theme reflecting the country’s current economic state.

View Event

Econsilium:

Econsilium, the nation’s largest consultancy-based business competition, challenges participants to solve complex business problems. It brings together individuals from diverse backgrounds to tackle real-world cases, showcasing their analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills. The event fosters innovation, collaboration, and networking, connecting participants with industry leaders and mentors while nurturing future business leaders.

View Event

Ascend:

Ascend, the club's flagship skill development initiative, features workshops and webinars aimed at enhancing members' expertise in core areas. The program fosters growth in functional domains while developing personal skills like graphic design, marketing, and research. Ascend is vital for member evaluations, significantly impacting their future opportunities within the club. Performance in this program serves as a key metric for assessing potential and shaping members' trajectories within the organization.

Chornicle is the most renowned research-based academic publication focusing on current social and economic challenges. It is spearheaded by the Young Economists’ Forum, the premier academic club at North South University. This platform showcases the exceptional knowledge and vision amassed by YEF's young researchers, academics, entrepreneurs, and professionals during their time in the club. Chornicle stands as a testament to YEF’s accomplishments, solidifying its reputation as a leading publication within the business education community, both locally and internationally.

While initial skepticism surrounded the release of a publication managed entirely by students, YEF has proven its capabilities by producing 13 successful editions of Chornicle. These editions satisfy the curiosity of eager readers, covering contemporary economic topics such as domestic and global economic growth, financial inclusion, policy-making challenges, money laundering, the economic impacts of climate change, and financial crimes, along with solutions to the identified issues.

The Young Economists’ Forum is driven by the ambition to lead Bangladesh’s business education community. YEF has always strived to elevate the reputation of North South University's School of Business and Economics, with Chornicle serving as the embodiment of that success. The publication highlights the outcomes of the club's flagship national events, workshops, and public lectures, making it the true crown jewel of YEF.
